Projects/Past Experinces

My junior year of high school (2016), I took a semester to intern at the Chicago Proton Center, which is a cutting-edge cancer research and treatment facility and one of only two dozen or so in the country. Under their direction, I aided them with desigining a series of plugins in Python that would aid them in sorting, searching, and acting upon the data of their patients. They were generally heavy on use of scientific Python libraries like pandas, numpy, etc. as well as regular expressions. In some form or another, my programming is likely still in use there to this day.

My Github can be found here for my personal projects. I suspect the Discord bot will be my first major undertaking, when I find the time for it. I may also have grander plans for the image manager, but that was a timed assignment.
